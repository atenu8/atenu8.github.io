Power Sponge Attenuator

The Power Sponge attenuator is a hand‑built, custom‑wired unit that gives you a second volume control for your tube amplifier. Dial in your tone and power tube warmth while keeping your sound at manageable levels.

  • Plug in and dial in the amount of speaker volume and tube warmth you desire.
  • Allows the amplifier to be opened up yet at lower sound levels.
  • Perfect for condos, bedroom sound levels and ideal for recording.
  • Compatible with 8 Ω, 4 Ω and 16 Ω outputs.
